About Us

About Us:

At LogitX, we are transforming the logistics industry by connecting businesses with the right logistics partners through a seamless, efficient, and technology-driven platform. Headquartered in Mysuru, Karnataka, LogitX serves as a comprehensive logistics aggregator, helping businesses navigate the complexities of supply chain management.

Our platform brings together top logistics companies offering multimodal transportation, multi-temperature control, real-time tracking, and customized loading and unloading solutions. With LogitX, businesses can easily explore, compare, and select logistics services that perfectly fit their needs

Our Vision:

To become the leading logistics aggregator, revolutionizing the global supply chain ecosystem by fostering seamless connectivity and operational efficiency

Our Mission:

To simplify logistics for businesses by providing an innovative platform that enables quick, reliable, and tailored solutions
